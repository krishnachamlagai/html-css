How to make Nepali Chiya (Tea)

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoon of tea leaves
  • 1 and 1/2 cup water
  • 1 small piece of ginger, sliced or crushed
  • 3 green cardamom pods, crushed
  • 2 cup whole milk
  • 3 tablespoon sugar, per taste
  • a pinch of crushed black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat water with spices for about 3-5 minutes.
  2. Add tea leaves, sugar and bring it to boil until it reaches brown color.
  3. Reduce the heat, then add milk and bring it back to boil
  4. Pour tea using a strainer then, serve it hot.
